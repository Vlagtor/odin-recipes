Authentic Italian Lasagne

Home

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's a hearty and comforting meal, perfect for family gatherings or a cozy dinner.

This recipe features a flavorful tomato-based sauce with ground beef, aromatic herbs, and a hint of garlic, layered between tender pasta sheets and a creamy ricotta mixture. Baked to perfection, the golden, cheesy top adds the perfect finishing touch. Serve it with a side of garlic bread or a fresh salad for a complete meal!

Ingredients for 6 servings

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 small celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 300g (10.5 oz) ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 100ml (⅓ cup) red wine (optional)
  • 400g (14 oz) canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp nutmeg (optional)
  • 250ml (1 cup) beef broth
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

For the Béchamel Sauce:

  • 50g (3.5 tbsp) unsalted butter
  • 50g (⅓ cup) all-purpose flour
  • 500ml (2 cups) whole milk
  • ¼ tsp nutmeg
  • ½ tsp salt

For the Layers:

  • 250g (9 oz) fresh lasagna sheets (or no-boil lasagna noodles)
  • 200g (7 oz) mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 100g (3.5 oz) Parmigiano-Reggiano, grated

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté the onion, carrot, and celery until soft. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  2. Add the ground beef (and pork if using) and cook until browned. Pour in the red wine (if using) and let it evaporate.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, pepper, nutmeg, and beef broth. Simmer on low heat for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Make the Béchamel Sauce: In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour and cook for about 1 minute.
  5. Gradually add the milk, whisking continuously to avoid lumps. Cook until thickened. Season with salt and nutmeg, then set aside.
  6. Assemble the Lasagna: Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer lasagna sheets, followed by more meat sauce, béchamel sauce, and a sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmigiano-Reggiano. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with béchamel and cheese on top.
  8. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 15-20 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  9. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
